HAB Open Data Project

An open data initiative from Queensland, Australia

Welcome to the Australian High Altitude Balloon (HAB) Open Data Project.

Our goal is to make flight data and images from high altitude balloons publicly available to all Australians to improve launch safety and awareness of science and technology by making real world data available for analysis and comparison.

This website is still under construction.
