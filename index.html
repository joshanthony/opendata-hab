Welcome to the Australian High Altitude Balloon (HAB) Open Data Project.

Our goal is to make flight data and images from high altitude balloons publicly available to all Australians to improve launch safety and awareness of aeronautical science and technology through making real world data available for review, analysis and comparison.

This website is still under construction. Our first dataset is now available after our first launch on 25 June 2016. Please download the dataset above and watch the highlight reel on YouTube.

Contact us

If you're planning your own launch you're welcome to email us: hab at outora dot com and we'll do our best to give you an informative answer or point you in the right direction.
