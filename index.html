Welcome to the Australian High Altitude Balloon (HAB) Open Data Project.

Our goal is to make flight data and images from high altitude balloons available to all Australians for their better understanding of science and technology and improved launch safety and awareness through the availability of real world data for analysis and comparison.

This website is still under construction.
